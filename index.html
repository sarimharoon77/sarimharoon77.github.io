Work

AquaWatch

A brief overview of our main goals for this project I am developing AquaWatch Pakistan, a data driven web platform focused on monitoring groundwater depletion. The project integrates open satellite data from NASA GRACE with global water stress datasets to visualize invisible groundwater trends at the district level. Using interactive maps and dashboards, the platform translates complex scientific data into accessible insights for communities. The project also explores basic predictive modeling to estimate future water scarcity risks based on historical trends. AquaWatch Pakistan is designed as an open source initiative to support data driven decision making in water resource management and climate resilience.

Taleem Tracker

A brief overview of our main goals for this project An interactive platform aimed at highlighting educational access gaps and literacy challenges across Pakistan. The project combines district level education data from independent surveys and public datasets. Through map based visualizations, users can explore literacy indicators. The platform also envisions a secure, anonymous community reporting feature to flag non functional schools.
